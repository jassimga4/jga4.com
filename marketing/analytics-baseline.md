# Analytics baseline (Plausible)

This site includes Plausible analytics via:
`data-domain="jassimgafoor.github.io"`.

## What to record (first 7–14 days)
Capture these numbers once daily (or at least on day 7 + day 14), then compute a simple average:

1. Total pageviews (site-wide)
2. Unique visitors
3. Top referrers (referral domains)
4. Top pages (by views)
5. Device split (mobile vs desktop, if available)

## How to use it
After you post for 4–6 weeks, compare the same metrics again. Your goal is to see:
1. Referral domains shifting toward higher-quality sources (LinkedIn, GitHub, Google)
2. Case studies/blog pages getting a larger share of views
