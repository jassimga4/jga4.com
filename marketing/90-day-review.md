# 90-day marketing review checklist

Use this on day 90 after starting the plan. If you can, do a quick check on day 45 to avoid drifting.

## 1) Snapshot your baseline (from `marketing/analytics-baseline.md`)

Record:

1. Pageviews (site-wide)
2. Unique visitors
3. Top referrers
4. Top pages
5. Mobile vs desktop (if available)

## 2) Compare “after” vs baseline

For each metric below, decide: improving, flat, or declining.

1. Total pageviews and unique visitors
2. Share of referrals from “high-intent” sources (LinkedIn, GitHub)
3. Views on:
   - `case-study.html`
   - `blog.html`
   - `contact.html`

## 3) Content performance review

Answer:

1. Which post/case study link drove the most sessions?
2. Which page produced the most “contact intent” (visits to `contact.html`)?
3. Do you have at least one piece of content you can reuse as a monthly “anchor”?

## 4) Action plan (what to do next)

Choose one:

1. Double down: update the best-performing case study with clearer outcomes and add 1–2 extra bullet points.
2. Fix the gap: if people visit case studies but don’t click contact, rewrite the CTA section on `contact.html` to be more specific to their needs.
3. Produce one new “anchor” post: publish one new blog post that supports the best LinkedIn angle you found.

## 5) Posting cadence adjustment

If referral quality improved, keep biweekly.

If referrals are flat, reduce to one post/week for two weeks and make each post more targeted (pick one audience pain point and repeat it).
